About Ofak Trading Company

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Ofak Trading Company began with a simple mission: to make shipping between China and Ghana easier, faster, and more reliable for Ghanaian businesses.

What started as a small logistics company in Tesano, Accra has grown into one of Ghana's leading shipping specialists for China-Ghana trade.

Over the past 15 years, we've shipped over 5,000 containers and built strong relationships with ports, customs officials, and transport providers in both countries.
