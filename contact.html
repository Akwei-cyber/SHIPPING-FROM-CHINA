Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping from China to Ghana take?

Sea freight typically takes 35-45 days, while air freight takes 5-10 days depending on the service level.

What documents do I need for shipping?

Commercial invoice, packing list, bill of lading/airway bill, and certificate of origin are typically required.

Do you handle customs clearance in Ghana?

Yes, we provide complete customs clearance services for all shipments.

What is your minimum shipment size?

We handle everything from small packages (air freight) to full container loads (sea freight).
